I serve as a teaching assistant/instructor for **Circuits Laboratory**.

Main responsibilities include:

- Designing and explaining lab experiments on basic circuits, measurement, and instrumentation
- Guiding students on using lab equipment (power supplies, DMMs, etc.)
- Answering questions during lab sessions and holding office hours
- Grading lab reports and providing feedback on experimental procedures and data analysis
